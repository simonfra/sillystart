Disc pain

1: Has there been a fall/RTA/trauma? No = go 2

2: Has there been sudden onset of low back pain following an action of lifting/ twisting? Yes = go to 3

3: Bending forwards, is it painful? Yes = go to 6

6: Is the patient standing with a lateral pelvic shift? Yes = go to 7

7: Palpate Lumbar segments, is it painful? Yes = go to 8

8: Sitting slouched, straighten patientâ€™s leg, lift toes, and bend head forward.
Is it painful or is altered sensation felt? Yes = go to 9

9: Therapist lifts painful leg straight up into the air. Is it painful or is altered sensation felt?
Patient may lift head to test further. Yes = go to 10

10: Test reflexes, myotomes, and dermatomes. Are some positive? Yes = Disc pain

Lumbar stenosis

1: Has there been a fall/RTA/trauma? No = go 2

2: Has there been sudden onset of low back pain following an action of lifting/ twisting? No = go 11

11: Palpate Lx segments, is it painful? Yes = go 12

12: Is patient over 50yrs old? Yes = go 13

13: When walking up hill or bent forward, is it less painful? No = Lumbar stenosis

Lumbar

Muscle spasm/ strain

1: Has there been a fall/RTA/trauma? No = go 2

2: Has there been sudden onset of low back pain following an action of lifting/ twisting? Yes = go 3

3: Bending forwards, is it painful? No = go 4

4: Bending backwards, is it painful? No = go 5

5: Bending to the right and left, is it painful? Yes = go 14

14: Is increased muscle tension evident in muscles along back? Yes = go 15

15: Feel the muscle bellies along the lower spine and above the pelvis is it painful to touch? Yes = Muscle spasm/ strain

Myofascial tightness
